Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for StreetBite?

Signing up for StreetBite is easy! Simply click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the prompts to create your account.

Can I use StreetBite in multiple locations?

Yes, StreetBite is available in multiple locations. You can use it wherever our service is supported.

What types of vendors are available on StreetBite?

StreetBite offers a wide variety of vendors, including food stalls, restaurants, cafes, food trucks, and more. You'll find something for every taste!

How does the rating system work?

Users can rate vendors and leave reviews based on their experiences. These ratings and reviews help other users make informed decisions when choosing a vendor.

What payment methods are accepted?

StreetBite accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile payment apps, and cash on delivery. The available payment methods may vary depending on the vendor.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through the "Contact" section of our website or app. We're here to help with any questions or issues you may have!
